## 5. Photography & Visual Standards

**This is the #1 most critical improvement for your shop.** Your product images are currently `null` (no images). Without high-quality photos, no amount of SEO or design will drive sales.

### Image Requirements per Listing

Etsy allows up to 10 photos + 1 video per listing. **Use at least 7 photos per listing:**

| Photo # | Purpose | Example |
|---|---|---|
| **1 (Hero)** | Clean product shot on white/neutral background | The main "scroll-stopping" image |
| **2** | Product in use / lifestyle context | Person wearing the bag, candle lit in living room |
| **3** | Scale/size reference | Product next to common object or being held |
| **4** | Close-up detail | Texture, stitching, material quality |
| **5** | What's in the box | All included items laid out flat |
| **6** | Infographic | Key features/dimensions overlaid on image |
| **7** | Gift presentation | Product in gift box/bag (for gift-related tags) |
| **8-10** | Additional angles, color options, or lifestyle shots |

### Photography Tips

- **Background:** White or light neutral for hero shot; styled for lifestyle shots
- **Lighting:** Natural daylight is best; avoid harsh shadows
- **Resolution:** Minimum 2000 x 2000 px (Etsy recommends this for zoom feature)
- **Aspect Ratio:** 4:3 or 1:1 (square is safest for mobile)
- **Consistency:** Use the same lighting, background, and editing style across all listings
- **Add a Video:** Even a simple 15-second video boosts listing quality score significantly

### Low-Budget Photography Setup

- Smartphone camera (iPhone/Samsung in good lighting)
- $20 poster board for white background
- Natural window light (shoot during golden hour)
- Free editing with Snapseed or Lightroom Mobile
